Welcome to My Portfolio

Introduction

Throughout this site, you'll find a collection of projects that showcase my learning progression, from my first steps in Python to creating interactive games and applications. Each project represents not just code, but lessons learned, challenges overcome, and the satisfaction of bringing ideas to life through programming.

Whether you're here to learn about my work, understand my approach to problem-solving, or simply explore what I've created, I invite you to take a look around. Feel free to check out my projects, read about my experiences, and discover what drives my passion for computer science.

Future Goals & Interests

My primary goal is to deepen my understanding of code and programming so that I can help others thrive and grow in their creativity and problem-solving skills. I believe that technology is a powerful tool for empowering others to think critically, innovate, and bring their ideas to life.
