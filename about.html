About Me

Who I Am

I’m Zachary, a student developer who loves exploring how things work—especially through programming and video game design. I enjoy creating my own games, experimenting with new ideas, and learning the logic behind the systems that shape the digital world.

Outside of coding, I enjoy spending time with family and friends, playing games, watching TV, and having meaningful conversations about God’s Word and growing in my faith in Jesus Christ.

Technology Interests

My primary focus is programming and software development. I’m especially interested in Python and the many ways it can be used to automate tasks, solve problems, and build creative projects. I enjoy exploring different logical approaches to coding and learning how various engineering disciplines apply computational thinking.

Video game development is one of my biggest passions—not just writing the code, but understanding the mechanics, systems, and storytelling that bring games to life.

Programming & Creative Development

I aim to continuously build more complex programs using an incremental development process. Each project helps me grow as a problem‑solver and gives me opportunities to share what I’m learning with others. I hope to create an environment where fellow developers can collaborate, learn, and grow together.

Long‑Term Goals

In the future, I hope to work in computer science in multiple capacities—whether as a programmer, an educator teaching others to code, or a content creator sharing knowledge on YouTube. I also feel called to become a part‑time counselor. Having personally navigated OCD, I want to support others who face similar challenges and help them find healing and growth.

My faith plays a central role in my journey. I want to integrate my values and relationship with Jesus Christ into my work, using my skills to make a positive impact on others.
