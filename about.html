About Me

Interests & Hobbies

I am deeply fascinated by video games, their underlying design, and understanding the mechanics of how things work in our world today. In my free time, I love to combine these passions by coding my own games and playing them.

Technology Interests

My primary tech focus is centered on programming and software development. I am particularly interested in exploring the diverse ways Python can be used to automate and complete tasks, while continuously diving deeper into the language's standard libraries to master its foundational functions.
